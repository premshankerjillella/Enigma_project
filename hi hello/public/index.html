The Enigma Project

Hello and welcome to The Enigma Project!

This is a project started after realizing the magnitude of food wastage in the modern world
Here, we have a network of pick up vans in a locality that collect the leftover food from your house
and other houses in your locality and donate it to the people who need it more.
We give you an opprtunity to not waste the daily leftover food and give it to the needy through
this user friendly website where you can call in the food pickup car just by a click on you mouse!
You can save somebody's life by the following these 3 simple steps steps:

  • Click the donate button at the end of this page
  • Enter your name,phonenumber and address(these will not be shared with enyone else!)
  • Click the " submit " button to place your order
And thats it! our driver will come and pickup the food you have!

but the following minimum requirements have to be satisfied:

  • your food must not be stale
  • you shall be banned from this programme if it is found that
    you donate stale/poisoned ( knowingly or unknowingly ) food.

So dont miss this golden opportunity to light up someone's life :)
